What we do ?

Education

Education is the most important right of every child in India. We provide free education to children who cannot afford to pay fees in school and colleges.These are mostly children belonging to slum areas.We provide them the necessary books and stationary material.

Save Earth

Environment is one the most crucial part of human life. We take out campaigns to plant trees and also make it important that the sapling grows into a big tree.Green environment makes every human to feel joyful and calm.

Senior Citizens

"Old is Gold". This proverb certainly holds good for the senior citizens of India. We help them by providing the required medical facilities.We have also build an old age home for the old people who dont have anyone to care of them. In this home all the senior citizens live happily together.

Medical Help

We provide medical assistance free of cost for the poor people. Many a times these people are suffering from a major diseases like cancer,polio etc and they cant afford to cure themselves.So we people provide them the medical aid and also the medical fees for their cure.
